Barangay Mambaling is a vibrant community located in Cebu City, known for its strong partnership with the University of Cebu – Mandaue Extension (UC-METC) CARES. Together, they work on various outreach initiatives, disaster response programs, and development projects aimed at improving the lives of the residents. With a growing population, Barangay Mambaling is committed to providing its constituents with modern facilities, dedicated services, and opportunities for growth. The barangay is known for its progressive mindset, constantly striving to uplift the community through collaboration and continuous development. Whether it’s through community service, education, or infrastructure improvements, Barangay Mambaling remains dedicated to fostering a better future for its people.
